Mozzarella Sticks Recipe

Description

Popular in fast food restaurants, making mozzarella sticks is easy at home. These breaded cheese sticks are a simple and cheap alternative to eating out.

Ingredients

  • 6 Cheese sticks (optionally cut in half)
  • 1 Tbsp salt
  • 1 Tbsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 cup Panko breadcrumbs
  • 1 large egg

Steps

  1. Combine the Panko, salt, and Italian seasoning in one wide bowl.
  2. Crack the egg into another wide bowl and beat gently.
  3. Begin coating the cheese sticks first in the egg, then in the Panko until completely coated.
  4. Place coated sticks in the freezer for one hour.
  5. After an hour, remove from freezer and repeat the coating process. Dip each stick into egg again and then into Panko mix. When finished coating each stick again, put them back into the freezer for one more hour.
  6. After an hour remove from freezer. Add sticks to air fryer and cook at 390 degrees Fahrenheit for 10-12 minutes. Cook in batches if you need to. When finished cooking, remove from air fryer and eat immediately.
